## 一句話結論

4M Defense 贏得以色列 Eastern Border Security Barrier 相關大規模排雷 competitive tender，官方稱 initial / follow-on orders expected over `$50M` in program value，且屬 Israel `$1.7B` Eastern Border Security Barrier project 的一部分。這讓 4M 從 acquisition thesis 推進到 national-defense infrastructure execution platform；但 `$50M+` 不是已全額 PO、已全額 backlog、recognized revenue 或 cash collection。

## 事件摘要

大叔 2026-04-06 Substack Note 轉述 Ondas 官方新聞稿，稱 Ondas 旗下 4M Defense 贏得一項價值超過 `$50M` 的大規模排雷項目競標，該項目與以色列國防部牽頭的 `$1.7B` Eastern Border plan 有關。

官方新聞稿的措辭較精確：4M Defense won a competitive tender to execute demining work for a lead contractor to the Israel Ministry of Defense，as part of Israel's `$1.7B` Eastern Border Security Barrier project。Initial and follow-on orders are expected to represent over `$50M` in program value。
